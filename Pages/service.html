Mechanical Seciton

The Mechanical Section at Al-Ama Limited plays a crucial role in ensuring the efficient and smooth operation of our construction projects. Our dedicated team of skilled professionals is responsible for the installation, maintenance, and repair of various mechanical systems within our residential, commercial, and industrial projects. Key Responsibilities: Plumbing Systems: Design and installation of water supply and drainage systems, including pipes, fittings, fixtures, and pumps. Maintenance and repair of plumbing systems to prevent leaks and ensure optimal functionality. HVAC Systems: Design and installation of heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems, including ductwork, fans, and control systems. Regular maintenance and calibration of HVAC systems to optimize energy efficiency and indoor air quality. Troubleshooting and repair of HVAC equipment to minimize downtime and ensure comfort. Fire Protection Systems: Installation and maintenance of fire sprinkler systems, fire alarms, and smoke detectors. Regular testing and inspection of fire safety equipment to ensure compliance with local codes and regulations. Piping Systems: Installation and maintenance of various piping systems, including those for water, gas, steam, and compressed air. Proper insulation and support of piping systems to prevent heat loss and ensure structural integrity. Pump Systems: Selection, installation, and maintenance of pumps for water supply, drainage, and other applications. Regular inspection and testing of pumps to ensure optimal performance and energy efficiency. Our Commitment: At Al-Ama Limited, we are committed to delivering high-quality mechanical installations and services that meet or exceed industry standards. Our team of experienced professionals utilizes the latest technologies and techniques to ensure the efficient and reliable operation of all mechanical systems.
