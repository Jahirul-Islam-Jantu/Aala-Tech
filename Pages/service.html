Mechanical Seciton

The Mechanical Section at Al-Ama Limited plays a crucial role in ensuring the efficient and smooth operation of our construction projects. Our dedicated team of skilled professionals is responsible for the installation, maintenance, and repair of various mechanical systems within our residential, commercial, and industrial projects. Key Responsibilities: Plumbing Systems: Design and installation of water supply and drainage systems, including pipes, fittings, fixtures, and pumps. Maintenance and repair of plumbing systems to prevent leaks and ensure optimal functionality. HVAC Systems: Design and installation of heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems, including ductwork, fans, and control systems. Regular maintenance and calibration of HVAC systems to optimize energy efficiency and indoor air quality. Troubleshooting and repair of HVAC equipment to minimize downtime and ensure comfort.
