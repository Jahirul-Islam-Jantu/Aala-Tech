Who We Are?

Our Mission Is To Become The Best Company

Al-Ama Limited is a premier construction company based in the heart of Al Khobar, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Conveniently located on King Abdul Aziz Road at the bustling 16th Street in the Suvekha Area, we specialize in delivering high-quality construction services tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients. With a steadfast commitment to excellence, Al-Ama Limited focuses on the construction of homes, office spaces, and apartments, ensuring that every project reflects our dedication to craftsmanship, safety, and sustainability.
